|---|---|

| H3 | 24 / 30 |
| Body | 16 / 24 |
| Small | 13 / 20 |

## Component rules
- Max content width 1280.
- 12-column layout rhythm.
- Radius: cards 14, inputs 10, hero 18.
- Tabular numerals for monetary and time values.
- Motion is subtle and disabled under `prefers-reduced-motion`.
